4.3. Preprocessing

4.3.1. Hair Removal Method

The hair removal process used a morphological blackhat operation with a defined kernel size of 9 by 9 using MORPH_RECT structuring element. The operation targeted dark elongated structures on lighter skin backgrounds to isolate hair strands effectively. A binary threshold was applied to the blackhat output to create a mask identifying hair regions across the image surface.

Inpainting with a radius of 6 using the INPAINT_TELEA algorithm filled the masked regions with surrounding pixel information. This restored the underlying skin texture beneath each removed hair strand. The process ran on every image in the dataset before any further preprocessing steps were applied. Output images showed clean lesion boundaries free from hair obstructions which improved downstream feature extraction quality.
