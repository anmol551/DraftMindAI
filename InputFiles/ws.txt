The web application is a Streamlit-based AI-powered loan risk and governance platform integrating a TabNet deep learning regression model with a business rule governance layer and LLM-based explanation generation. Libraries used include streamlit, pandas, numpy, joblib, pytorch-tabnet, openai, and base64. User inputs are collected via ten numeric input fields covering Annual Income, Credit Score, Debt-to-Income Ratio, Bankruptcy History, Previous Loan Defaults, Length of Credit History, Monthly Income, Net Worth, Interest Rate, and Total Debt-to-Income Ratio. On button click, the predict_loan_risk function applies six engineered features, scales selected columns using a pre-fitted StandardScaler loaded from scaler.pkl, and feeds the result to the saved TabNet model (tabnet_risk_model.zip) to produce a continuous risk score. A threshold-based business rule flags scores above 50 as High Risk and overrides the model to Rejected regardless of the raw model decision. The generate_llm_explanation function then calls OpenAI GPT-4o-mini with a structured prompt to produce a formal underwriting report covering risk interpretation, model versus governance analysis, financial strengths, risk drivers, improvement recommendations, and executive summary. The app displays predicted risk score, model decision, business flag, final decision, and the full AI-generated report inline.
