Dataset: Resume Dataset from Kaggle (snehaanbhawal/resume-dataset), originally sourced from livecareer.com.
Size: 2,484 resumes across 4 columns — ID, Resume_str, Resume_html, and Category.
Format: CSV with accompanying PDFs stored in category-labelled folders.
Categories (24): HR, Designer, Information-Technology, Teacher, Advocate, Business-Development, Healthcare, Fitness, Agriculture, BPO, Sales, Consultant, Digital-Media, Automobile, Chef, Finance, Apparel, Engineering, Accountant, Construction, Public-Relations, Banking, Arts, Aviation.
Class Distribution: Heavily imbalanced — IT and Business Development lead at 120 each; BPO is lowest at 22.
Quality: No missing values, no duplicates.
Preprocessing: ID and Resume_html columns dropped; Resume_str used as primary text source.
